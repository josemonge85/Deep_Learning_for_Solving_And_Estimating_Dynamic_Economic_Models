# Readings, Lecture 07 (B05): Automatic differentiation for DEQNs

Default policy: link only.

## Autodiff foundations

- **Baydin, Pearlmutter, Radul, Siskind (2018), "Automatic differentiation in machine learning: a survey,"** *JMLR*, 18. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/1502.05767). The survey that the autodiff exposition follows.
- **Griewank, Walther (2008),** *Evaluating Derivatives: Principles and Techniques of Algorithmic Differentiation*, 2nd edition, SIAM. [publisher](https://epubs.siam.org/doi/book/10.1137/1.9780898717761). The canonical reference on AD theory and implementation.

## Application to DEQN training

- **Azinovic, Gaegauf, Scheidegger (2022), "Deep Equilibrium Nets,"** *IER*. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1111/iere.12575). The autodiff treatment of equilibrium residuals in the lecture's IRBC notebook follows this paper.
