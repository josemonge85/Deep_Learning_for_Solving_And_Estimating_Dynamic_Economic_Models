## Selected references

### Taming the Curse of Dimensionality: Quantitative Economics with Deep Learning

- **Authors:** Fernandez-Villaverde, J., Nuno, G., Perla, J.
- **Year:** 2025
- **Venue:** NBER Working Paper / forthcoming
- **Link:** <https://www.nber.org/papers/w33117>
- **Notes:** Survey of deep-learning methods for quantitative economic modeling. Read alongside Lectures 02 and 06.

### Probabilistic Machine Learning: An Introduction

- **Authors:** Murphy, K. P.
- **Year:** 2022
- **Venue:** MIT Press
- **Link:** <https://probml.github.io/pml-book/book1.html>
- **Notes:** Free CC-BY-NC-ND draft from probml.github.io. Comprehensive textbook reference for the ML and deep-learning fundamentals.

### An Introduction to Statistical Learning, with Applications in R (2nd edition)

- **Authors:** James, G., Witten, D., Hastie, T., Tibshirani, R.
- **Year:** 2021
- **Venue:** Springer
- **Link:** <https://www.statlearning.com/>
- **Notes:** Free PDF available from statlearning.com. Accessible companion to ESL.
